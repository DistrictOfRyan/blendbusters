🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • Probiotics are strain-specific, not "more strains = better." A high strain count or big AFU number is marketing, not proof.
  • Most healthy adults aren’t advised to take a daily probiotic — the benefit is situational (e.g., after antibiotics).
  • Fermented foods + fiber (yogurt, kefir, sauerkraut, kimchi + a varied diet) is a legit, cheap way to feed your gut.
  • AFU vs CFU: Seed’s "53.6B AFU" is a different yardstick — don’t compare it head-to-head with a "25B CFU" label.
  • Consult a healthcare provider before switching, and pick the strain to your goal — identity matters more than count.
