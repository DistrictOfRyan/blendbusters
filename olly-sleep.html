🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • Melatonin is the only ingredient with real evidence here — best for sleep timing and jet lag. Plain, it costs about a dollar a month.
  • More melatonin isn’t better. Low doses (0.5–1mg) often work as well as 3–10mg, with less morning grogginess. DIY lets you dial it down.
  • OLLY’s L-theanine is 50mg — half the 100–200mg used in studies. The calming herbs are a pinch below any tested amount.
  • They’re candy — ~2g of added sugar a serving, right before bed.
  • Melatonin fixes timing, not every sleep problem. If anxiety or apnea is the cause, no gummy will help.
