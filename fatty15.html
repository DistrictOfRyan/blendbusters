🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • fatty15 is one ingredient — 100mg of purified pentadecanoic acid (C15:0). No blend, no cofactors.
  • C15:0 is naturally in whole/full-fat dairy — roughly 100mg in a glass of whole milk, ~90mg an ounce of cheddar.
  • It’s also a plain commodity chemical (CAS 1002-84-2), now sold as generic capsules for a third of fatty15’s price.
  • The evidence is early and largely company-funded — mostly preclinical, with an independent analysis finding no causal heart benefit. Nobody should treat it as proven.
  • The $50 is buying the branding, not exclusive access to the molecule.
