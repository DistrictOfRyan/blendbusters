🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • ARMRA is one ingredient: grass-fed bovine colostrum powder. Nothing exotic, nothing proprietary about the ingredient itself.
  • "Cold-Chain BioPotent" is a processing name, not a special ingredient — all colostrum is minimally processed and spray- or freeze-dried.
  • The "400+ bioactive nutrients" (IgG, lactoferrin, growth factors) are naturally in any quality colostrum, not unique to ARMRA.
  • The human evidence is weak-to-moderate — some gut-barrier and athlete-immunity signals from small studies, done on generic colostrum, not ARMRA.
  • Same ingredient, roughly 11x the price. A plain tub runs about $10/mo at the same dose.
