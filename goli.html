🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • One tablespoon of real ACV is ~15,000mg of liquid. A 500mg gummy serving is a sliver — you’d eat a whole bottle to match one spoonful.
  • The label says up to six a day, which drains a $19 bottle in about 10 days. That’s the real monthly cost.
  • They’re candy — ~2g of added sugar per serving, and acid on your teeth all day is the opposite of tooth-friendly.
  • The beetroot and pomegranate are micrograms — coloring, not a "superfood" dose.
  • Evidence for the marketed benefits is weak either way. Dilute real ACV in water (or take a plain capsule) and pocket the difference.
