🔥 Real talk: what actually works

We'd rather keep you than sell you hype. Straight from the research:

  • Zinc and Vitamin D do the heavy lifting, but mostly only if you're deficient. Fix a real deficiency and testosterone can genuinely respond. Already normal? Don't expect much.
  • Tongkat Ali and fenugreek, promising but small/mixed human studies. Possibly helpful, not proven.
  • Shilajit, boron, taurine, thin human evidence for testosterone. Cheap, low-stakes, manage expectations.
  • Vitamin K1/K2, good for bone and mood, does essentially nothing for testosterone. It's on the label for you, not for your T.
  • The real move: a blood test tells you if you're actually low. Worth more than any bottle, this stack or theirs.

About

Why BlendBusters exists

Proprietary supplement blends hide underdosed ingredients behind big markups and bigger promises. BlendBusters does one thing well: take an expensive blend, show you exactly what's inside and what it really costs, and hand you an honest, cheaper way to get the same ingredients. We make money through affiliate links when you buy the cheaper option we recommend, never by selling you the hype.

Our method

We match ingredient doses 1:1 to the product's own label, price each ingredient at typical US bulk retail, and rate the evidence for each from published human research, favoring transparency over marketing. Prices are approximate and change over time. BlendBusters is a project of Hunt Web Consulting Services (Kentucky, USA).
