🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • Electrolytes genuinely matter for heavy sweating, keto, or extended fasting — real sodium loss plain water won’t replace.
  • For a normal diet and normal activity, a $1.50 stick offers little your food doesn’t already supply.
  • LMNT is deliberately high-sodium (1,000 mg). That’s 2–3× most competitors — great for athletes, risky for others.
  • Sodium caution: if you have high blood pressure, kidney, or heart issues, or take BP/potassium meds, talk to a doctor before high-sodium electrolytes.
  • The dupe is nearly free — the actives are salt, a salt substitute, and a magnesium powder.
