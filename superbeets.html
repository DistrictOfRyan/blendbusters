🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • The active is nitrate from beetroot — present in ordinary beet powder just as much as the "fermented" kind.
  • The nitrate content isn’t disclosed — the one number that actually drives the effect. You’re paying for branding, not a verified dose.
  • The vitamin C (50mg) and magnesium (18mg) are trivial — a single orange has more vitamin C. Any diet or multivitamin covers them.
  • Dietary nitrate has moderate evidence for blood pressure and exercise — dose-dependent, and eating beets or leafy greens works too.
  • Bulk beet powder is the same red powder for a tenth of the price. One kilo tub lasts about half a year.
