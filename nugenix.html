🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • The proprietary blend hides the doses, so you can’t tell how much fenugreek you’re actually getting. Buy it separately and you can.
  • The zinc is 1 mg — the RDA is 11 mg and studies use 25–30 mg. It’s a token amount that does nothing meaningful.
  • Citrulline and tribulus are there for the label — citrulline is a pump ingredient, tribulus has no human T evidence.
  • Fenugreek is the one moderately-studied ingredient, and a standalone extract costs about a fifth of Total-T.
  • The real move: test your vitamin D and zinc. If you’re not low, neither this nor the swap will move much.
