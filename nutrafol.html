🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • The "Synergen Complex" is a proprietary blend — 2,190mg total, so you can’t see how much saw palmetto, ashwagandha, or biotin you’re actually getting.
  • Biotin only helps hair if you’re deficient, which is rare — and it can skew some lab tests. It’s the marketing hero with the weakest case.
  • Saw palmetto is the marquee ingredient, with weak-to-moderate evidence as an adjunct — far weaker than the actual hair drugs.
  • Marine collagen is likely most of the blend by weight, and it’s a commodity you can buy on its own for a fraction.
  • The genuinely proven option isn’t even in the bottle: OTC topical minoxidil runs ~$10–20/mo with real clinical evidence.
