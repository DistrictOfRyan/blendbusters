🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • The active is soluble fiber — a commodity. Generic psyllium or a plain fiber supplement delivers the same, for a fraction of the price.
  • Consistency and hydration do the real work — taking fiber daily with plenty of water matters far more than the brand on the bottle.
  • The probiotic side is legit but generic — a standalone multi-strain lets you see the CFU count and costs less.
  • You’re paying for discretion and a nice gummy, not a better result. The formula isn’t special; the packaging is.
  • Start low and build up — too much fiber too fast causes gas and bloating. That’s true of any fiber, branded or not.
