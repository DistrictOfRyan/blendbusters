Key findings

BlendBusters analysis
  • Across 206 popular supplements, the typical (median) brand product is priced 2.9x its lower-cost, ingredient-matched alternative; the average markup is 5.3x.
  • 76% of the supplements analyzed are priced at least 2x the ingredient-matched alternative, and 60 are priced 5x or more.
  • The largest single gap is Isagenix IsaLean Shake: about $296/month versus a roughly $70/month ingredient-matched alternative, an estimated $2,712/year in savings.
  • The steepest multiple is Amway Nutrilite Double X, priced about 42x its lower-cost ingredient match.
  • On average a tracked brand product costs $55/month versus about $18/month for its ingredient-matched alternative.
  • In aggregate the 206 brand products would cost about $136,296/year; the ingredient-matched alternatives total about $44,311/year.

Every figure is estimated from public retail prices and derives from the 206 live comparisons on this site. A “lower-cost ingredient match” shares overlapping ingredients and a similar intended use; it is not a medically equivalent product. This is a price comparison, not medical advice.
