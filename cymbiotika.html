🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • The active is vitamin C — ascorbic acid is one of the cheapest bulk ingredients in the entire supplement industry.
  • Liposomal delivery has modest evidence — small trials show ~1.3–1.8x higher blood levels, but simply taking a bit more plain vitamin C reaches similar levels for pennies.
  • The fruit extracts and bamboo silica are token amounts — the story is still 1,000mg of vitamin C.
  • 1,000mg is far above the ~75–90mg RDA; excess water-soluble vitamin C is largely excreted anyway.
  • Want the exact liposomal format? A cheaper brand (e.g. Codeage) matches it at roughly half the price.
