🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • "2,240 mg of mushrooms" is misleading — that’s four species plus the oat substrate they’re grown on, split four ways. Each lands well under study doses.
  • A real fruiting-body extract at ~2 g of one mushroom beats MUD’s entire blend on actual potency — and the DIY build lets you do that.
  • The mushroom evidence is early and mixed — lion’s mane has small cognitive signals, the rest is mostly preclinical. Dose is everything.
  • ~35 mg of caffeine is about a third of a coffee — a gentle lift, not a replacement if you want the kick.
  • You’re paying for flavor, convenience, and branding. The cacao and chai are legit and cheap; the "functional" promise is where skepticism belongs.
