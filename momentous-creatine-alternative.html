Safety & who should check with a professional

Safety note

Creatine monohydrate is generally well tolerated in healthy adults at ~3–5 g/day; mild GI upset or water-weight gain can occur.

  • Talk to a physician or registered dietitian first if you have kidney disease or reduced kidney function, are pregnant or nursing, or take medication.
