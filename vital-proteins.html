🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • Collagen is a commodity — bovine-hide peptides. The powder in a $45 tub is chemically identical to bulk peptides at a third of the price.
  • There’s no proprietary blend here to justify the markup. You’re paying for grass-fed branding and packaging.
  • The "+ Vitamin C and Hyaluronic Acid" upsell just bolts on pennies of ascorbic acid — buy it separately for ~$1/mo.
  • Evidence is at best moderate for skin, and weaker for joints, hair, and nails.
  • Collagen is an incomplete protein (low in tryptophan) — don’t count it toward your full daily protein needs.
