🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • Huel isn’t a scam — it’s fully transparent and a genuinely balanced, convenient meal. The teardown here is purely price per meal.
  • "Complete nutrition" is bulk pea/rice protein, oats, flax/MCT, and an added vitamin panel — a multivitamin covers that last part.
  • Against subscription pricing the markup is only moderate (~32%); against retail it’s ~52%. Either way, DIY wins.
  • Whole food is fine too — eggs, oats, beans, yogurt. Huel’s value is speed and portability, not superiority.
  • The trade-off is five minutes of mixing. If you won’t touch a scoop, a turnkey like Soylent still beats Huel on price.
