🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • Credit where due: TestoFuel discloses every dose — no hidden blend. The problem is the amounts.

  • D-aspartic acid looked promising early, but later trials mostly came back null — especially in trained men.
  • Fenugreek, ginseng, oyster, K2 are token or underdosed here, and thin on testosterone evidence anyway.
  • The real move: a blood test for D and zinc. If you’re not low, neither this nor the swap will move much.
