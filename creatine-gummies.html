🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • Creatine monohydrate has strong evidence — one of the most-studied supplements for strength and performance. The molecule works; the format is the problem.
  • Independent testing ("Gummygate") found many creatine gummies contain far less than labeled — one delivered ~0.005g of a claimed 5g. Creatine degrades to creatinine under the heat and water used to make gummies.
  • The powder is the same Creapure molecule — 5g scooped into water, coffee, or a shake. No flavor tax, no stability roulette.
  • The gummy tax here is roughly 15–17x — about $53 a month for chewing instead of scooping.
  • Creatine is nearly tasteless and dissolves fine — there’s very little the gummy format actually buys you.
