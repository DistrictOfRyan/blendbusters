🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • The proprietary blends are the whole problem — you can’t tell if any active hits the dose used in research.
  • Bacopa is the best-studied ingredient, but Alpha Brain’s ~100 mg is well below the ~300 mg used in trials. The DIY dose fixes that.

  • Huperzine A and cat’s claw have weak-to-no cognitive evidence; they pad the label more than the effect.
  • Single ingredients let you tune the dose — the one thing a proprietary blend takes away.
