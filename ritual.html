🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • Ritual’s appeal is design and traceability, not unique nutrients — every ingredient is available cheaper elsewhere.
  • It deliberately omits iron and calcium, so it isn’t a complete multi anyway; a $12 USP multivitamin covers more.
  • The omega-3 (DHA) is the nicest touch — but a standalone algae or fish oil gives you more DHA per dollar.
  • Methylated folate and B12 are good forms, and plenty of generic multis now use them too.
  • If you eat a varied diet, a basic multi plus omega-3 is all most people need — no subscription required.
