🔥 Real talk: what actually works

  • Electrolytes genuinely help when you’re actually losing fluids — heavy sweat, illness, heat. That part is real.
  • But the "active" is sugar + sodium co-transport — oral-rehydration science from the 1970s. The inputs cost pennies.
  • About 11g of each stick is added sugar — meaningful if you’re sipping it casually all day.
  • The added B vitamins and vitamin C look nice on the label; most people aren’t deficient and just excrete the excess.
  • For a normal day, plain water does the job — and skips the sugar.
