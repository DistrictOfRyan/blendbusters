How close is the match?

BlendBusters analysis

Our match, physician’s choice 60 billion probiotic and probiotics (strain-specific), covers the overlapping ingredients behind 1MD Complete Probiotics Platinum at a comparable daily amount where the dose is disclosed. Here is what lines up, and what does not.

What matches

  • ✓A similar intended use, both are once-daily multi-strain probiotic capsules for digestive/gut support.
  • ✓Overlapping approach, both pair a multi-strain blend with a prebiotic fiber and include common Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium species.
  • ✓Physician’s Choice 60 Billion is a named, lower-cost ingredient match at a comparable-or-higher disclosed total CFU (60B vs 51B).

What differs

  • ≠Probiotic effects are strain-specific, the two use different strain rosters, and a higher total CFU does not equal an equivalent effect, so they’re not interchangeable.
  • ≠Both disclose only a blend total (no per-strain CFU), an important limitation for matching a specific strain.
  • ≠1MD is DTC with subscription markup and bundles a dietitian-consult perk; the match is mass retail.
